About WinnerGo

Your trusted transportation partner across Ethiopia

Our Story

Founded in 2018, WinnerGo began with a vision to transform Ethiopia's transportation system. What started as a small team in Addis Ababa has grown into a national network serving over 1 million passengers annually.

We partner with 50+ transport operators to connect 45+ cities across Ethiopia, providing reliable, comfortable journeys with real-time tracking and easy booking.

Our Mission & Values

Mission

To revolutionize Ethiopian transportation through technology, making travel safer, more reliable, and accessible to all.

Customer First

We prioritize passenger comfort, safety, and satisfaction in every decision.

Innovation

Continuously improving through technology and creative solutions.

Partnership

Growing together with our network of transport operators.
